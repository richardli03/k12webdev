Paper Prototyping

Middle, High

Art, Engineering

1 hour

E4E

To be an entrepreneur, designer, or engineer, you need to get your ideas out onto the page! In this workshop you will learn sketching skills such as how to use multiple line weights, what is isometric view, how to draw motion, and more that will be useful at any level!

This workshop works well with the Paper Prototyping workshop.

Materials + Preparation

The Teacher's Guide provides instructions for teaching basic sketching techniques as a way to communicate ideas. No prior art experience required! The workshop can be taught online or in person. Either way, students will need access to paper and writing utensils.
