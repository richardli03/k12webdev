Scratch Art

Age: ELEMENTARY

Discipline: ARTS SCIENCE DESIGN

Materials: GATHER & GO

In this module, kids will create their own scratch art by coloring cardstock with crayons and then painting over their coloring with black paint. Blah Blah Blah Blah

Jump to Files

Materials + Preparation

Cardstock Crayons Tape Black acrylic paint Dish Soap Paint brushes

Science Connection

You can also introduce hydrophilic vs hydrophobic solutions because this module works best when you mix the paint with dish soap so that the water based paint sticks well to the oil/waxy crayons.

Background Concepts

The students should be familiar with basic locomotor skills and the ability to apply paint in a thin but consistent layer over a surface of undefined dimension but a textured enough surface to retain a complete finish of the paint after application.
