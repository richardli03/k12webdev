Paper Planes

Age: ELEMENTARY and Middle

Discipline: ELECTRICITY SCIENCE DESIGN

Materials: GATHER & GO

Get ready for paper airplanes to fly all over! In this module kids will learn how to make a classic paper airplane. After that they can make modifications to their plane and see how it changes the behavior. They can learn vocab works like lift, thrust and drag. When they have modified their plans they can have a competition.
