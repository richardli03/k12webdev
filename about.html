About Us

We are a collection of Olin College students and faculty dedicated to broadening access to STEM/STEAM for pre-college learners. One group is from a course called Mathematics and Engineering for Everyone designed by Professor Sarah Spence Adams and first offered in 2021. The second group is from Affordable Design and Entrepreneurship (ADE), an Olin capstone program. The ADE Community Development track has included a focus on K-12 STEAM education through its Shifting Rhythms program which has been advised by Professor Kofi Taha since 2014. Each group has different goals and methods, but both believe that broadening access to STEM is a social justice issue for many reasons, including for the paths STEM can provide towards socio-economic mobility.
