Scratch Art

Elementary, Middle

Art, Science

1-2 hours

Shifting Rhythms

In this module, kids will create their own scratch art by coloring cardstock with crayons and then painting over their coloring with black paint.

Materials + Preparation

Cardstock
Crayons
Tape
Black acrylic paint
Dish Soap
Paint brushes

Science Connection

You can also introduce hydrophilic vs hydrophobic solutions because this module works best when you mix the paint with dish soap so that the water based paint sticks well to the oil/waxy crayons.
