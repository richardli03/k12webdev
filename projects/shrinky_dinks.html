Shrinky Dinks

Elementary, Middle, High

Art, Science

Less than 1 hour

Shifting Rhythms

In this module kids will create shrinky dinks and get introduced to design ideas like brainstorming and sketching before diving into creating their own shrinky dinks. Kids can be creative with their drawing and with what the end product is (i.e. key chains, jewelry, or fun trinkets!)

Materials + Preparation

Textured shrinky dink paper
Colored Pencils
Hole Punch
Scratch Paper or Notebook
Scissors
Toaster Oven/Oven/or Heat gun
