Snow Globe

Elementary, Middle

Art, Engineering, Science

1-2 hours

Shifting Rhythms

In this module kids will create a clay sculpture and fix it to the inside of a small jar filled with water and 'snow' to make a snow globe.

Materials + Preparation

Toaster oven/oven
Glass Jar
Clay
Pom Poms
Spray Sealant
Popsicle Stick
Glitter Glue
Hot Glue Gun
Paint/Brushes(optional)
Sequins (optional)
Loose Glitter (optional)
