Electromagnets

Elementary

Discipline: ELECTRICITY SCIENCE DESIGN

Materials: GATHER & GO

Kids will create their own electromagnet by coiling wire around a metal nail and powering it with a battery - then they can see their electromagnet pick up little pieces of metal. The kids will learn about electricity and magnetism. Warning- the batteries can get hot!
