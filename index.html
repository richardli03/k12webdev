Resources for K12 STEAM Activities

This website gives access to curricular materials for hands-on Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics (STEAM) activities developed through two initiatives at Olin College: Mathematics and Engineering for Everyone (E4E), a course designed by Professor Sarah Spence Adams, and Shifting Rhythms, a track led by Professor Kofi Taha within Affordable Design and Entrepreneurship (ADE). We hope our curricular materials will be used to broaden access to STEAM activities in K12 classrooms, after-school programs, libraries, homes, and anywhere else around the world. All materials are free to use under the Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial_ShareAlike 4.0 International license.
