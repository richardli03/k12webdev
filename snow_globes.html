Snow Globe

Age: ELEMENTARY and Middle

Discipline: ELECTRICITY SCIENCE DESIGN

Materials: GATHER & GO

In this module kids will create a clay sculpture and fix it to the inside of a small jar filled with water and 'snow' to make a snow globe.
