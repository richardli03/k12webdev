Cow Evolution: Exponentials in Video Games

Middle, High

Math

Less than 1 hour

E4E

Description

Math is everywhere - especially in video games! We’ll take a look at how to make predictions and strategic choices in a super simple phone game called Cow Evolution. In part one, we’ll recognize that this game is fundamentally based off exponentials and use that observation to predict how long it takes to beat the game in its most simplistic form. We’ll also gain intuition for just how fast exponential functions grow. In part two, we’ll talk about modeling. What parts of the game can we extrapolate away and what parts do we need to keep to answer questions about game strategy? Students will see how to simplify a complex system to a solvable problem, and understand how to transfer the ideas to the video games they know and love.

This workshop works well with the workshops Carnival Games and Games with a 100% Win Strategy.

Materials + Preparation

The Teacher's Guide provides instructions for working through the hidden exponentials inside Cow Evolution through an online or in-person workshop. If teaching online, students would require internet connected devices. In person, a calculator could be helpful.
